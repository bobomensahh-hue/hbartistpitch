The Premise

Music built at the
intersection of craft
and technology.

Hermit & Baker is launching a suite of AI-assisted music projects — not to replace human artistry, but to push its edges. Each project is grounded in genuine creative vision, with defined aesthetics, sound worlds, and release strategies.

These are singles-first, streaming-ready artists designed to grow their audiences song by song, genre by genre.

Artist 01 / Folk · Psychedelic · Spiritual
Goodluck
Mjongo
"Oh, Where Have You Been?" — a voice that carries the memory of places you've never been.
Wanderer Storyteller Earthy Spiritual Vocalist

Goodluck Mjongo is a folk vocalist and oral historian. His music lives in the tradition of campfire storytelling, shaped by a psychedelic warmth reminiscent of Khruangbin — spacious, unhurried, and deeply textured. Each song is a journey, not just a song.

Artist 02 / R&B · Soul · Songwriting
Beau
Mason
Intimate, stylish, smooth — a young producer-songwriter whose songs are made to be claimed by other voices.
Intimate Stylish Smooth Producer Songwriter

Beau Mason is a songwriter's songwriter — his catalogue is an offering to artists. Polished R&B productions with lyrical depth, built for major artist placements, sync licensing, and song publishing. Every track is a song that another artist will want to call their own.

Artist 03 / Jazz · African Fusion · Instrumental
Asè
An instrumentalist ensemble at the crossroads of African jazz, brass tradition, and cross-genre exploration.
Jazz Brass Ensemble Guitar African Fusion Instrumental

Asè operates as a collective — guitar, brass, rhythm — building sonic bridges between West African music traditions and contemporary jazz experimentation. No vocals needed when the instruments carry the narrative. Positioned for global jazz festivals, editorial playlisting, and sync.

Release
Strategy.

All tracks are released as singles — a deliberate choice to build algorithm-friendly momentum across three separate artist pages, growing each audience independently while reinforcing the Hermit & Baker brand.

1
Phase One · Q2–Q3 2025

Introduce

  • Drop lead singles for all three artists
  • Goodluck Mjongo: Billy London
  • Beau Mason: Slow Down Killa
  • Asè: Ginger Shot
  • Focus: DSP profile creation, editorial pitching
  • Metadata, cover art, artist bios finalised
2
Phase Two · Q3–Q4 2025

Build

  • Bi-weekly single cadence across the roster
  • Playlist pitching — folk, R&B, jazz categories
  • Beau Mason songwriter placement outreach
  • Sync licensing submissions for Asè
  • Social content strategy per artist persona
3
Phase Three · Q1 2026

Establish

  • Bundle singles into EPs per artist
  • Performance / live events for Asè
  • Songwriter sessions for Beau Mason
  • Press and media roll-out for Goodluck Mjongo
  • Review revenue streams & expand roster
